Case Tools and RDBMS

CASE Tools and RDBMS Lab is well equipped with 72 PCs with Intel Core 2 Duo 2.2 GHz processor and 7 PCs with Core i3 2.66 GHz processor. CASE tools are installed which enable the students to design , develop and test projects following lifecycle models .Systems are loaded with Oracle RDBMS facilitating students to design and query databases . The lab also has 6 printers and scanners along with 25 webcams that cater to the requirements of both the staff as well as the students. This lab is centrally air conditioned and has 4 wireless 54Mbps access points.

Compiler Design and System Software

Compiler Design laboratory is equipped with 72 PCs with i3 3 rd Gen, 4 GB DDR3 RAM, 500 GB SATA2 HDD .Compiler design and Operating system which makes the students to learn about open source operating systems, Ubuntu and fedora and Students gain exposure to lexical and YACC tools. The students are also trained to implement different phases of compilation, scheduling algorithms and concepts pertaining to operating systems using C Programming Language .

PG Computer Centre

The PG Computer Centre is equipped with centrally air conditioned and is furnished with 25 PCs, all of which are IBM desktops , operated on Lenovo Core i3 processor. The lab has a high speed Wi-Fi facility and also has 2 dot matrix printers. This lab fosters the research interests of PG Students and facilitates them to develop solutions for various problems in Computer science.

Data Structures and Network Programming

Data Structures and Network Programming laboratory is well equipped with Lenovo M72e, Intel Core i3 3 rd Gen, 4 GB DDR3 RAM, 500 GB SATA2 HDD and is centrally air conditioned. It helps the students to enrich their programming abilities in C and Java and enables the students to implement different data structures. Also the Students are trained to simulate networks, implement various protocols and networking algorithms.

Programming Language Laboratory 1

Programming Laboratory 1 is equipped with 66 Lenovo M71, Intel Core i3 2 nd Gen 3.1 GHz, 4 GB DDR3 RAM, 500 GB SATA HDD. It provides the students with rich programming experience in various languages such as C, C++, JAVA, .NET.

Programming Language Laboratory 2

Programming Laboratory 2, facilitates open source programming and is outfitted with 60 Lenovo M72e, Intel Core i3 3 rd Gen, 4 GB DDR3 RAM, 500 GB SATA2 HDD. This lab also has 6 laser printers.
